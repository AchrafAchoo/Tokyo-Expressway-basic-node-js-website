Our Main Office Areas

Shinjuku

Shinjuku Station is one of the busiest railway stations in the world, located in the Shinjuku ward of Tokyo, Japan. It serves as a major hub for various train lines, including JR East, Odakyu Electric Railway, Keio Corporation, and Tokyo Metro. The station is surrounded by numerous shopping, entertainment, and business districts.

Katsushika

Katsushika is a special ward located in the northeastern part of Tokyo, Japan. It is known for its traditional atmosphere, historical sites, and beautiful parks. One of the most famous attractions in Katsushika is the Shibamata Taishakuten, a Buddhist temple with a history dating back to 1629.

Edogawa

Edogawa is a ward located in the eastern part of Tokyo, Japan. It is known for its family-friendly atmosphere, extensive parks, and riverside areas. One of the main attractions in Edogawa is the Edogawa Natural Zoo, which offers free admission and is home to a variety of animals.
